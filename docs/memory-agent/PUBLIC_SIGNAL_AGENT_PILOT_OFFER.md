# Public Signal Agent — pilot offer (one page)

**What it is:** A time-boxed pilot to connect **one slice** of your institutional memory to a **governed public output loop**—voice or chat ask, grounded answer, audience-specific outputs, signage-ready copy, saved assets, and **approved QR / mobile handoff** for visitors.

**Duration:** 4–6 weeks (calendar to be agreed with your team).

---

## Loop delivered in the pilot

Ask → Public output → Staff brief → Leadership insight → Signage draft → Generated asset queue → **Approve Public QR** → Mobile handoff (visitor-safe URL)

Governance is built in: drafts, explicit approval for public QR, no auto-publish, internal slices never exposed on the public handoff route.

---

## Deliverables

| # | Deliverable | Notes |
|---|-------------|--------|
| 1 | **Memory source audit** | What we connect first (for example alumni, artists, programs—one coherent slice). |

| 7 | **Governance setup** | Policy alignment with your comms and risk stakeholders (see [AI Public Output Governance](./AI_PUBLIC_OUTPUT_GOVERNANCE.md)). |
| 8 | **Demo training session** | Walkthrough of the frozen five-minute demo path for your team. |

---

## What success looks like at the end of the pilot

- Staff can **ask** the pilot memory slice and get a **grounded** answer.
- The same run produces **public**, **staff**, and **leadership** outputs plus a **signage draft**.
- A **saved asset** can be **approved for QR** and opens correctly on a **second device** (the client-demo unlock).
- Your team can **repeat the demo** without engineering in the room.
