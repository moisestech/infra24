This workshop treats AI as infrastructure you steer—not a replacement author. We work through pressure (what the room feels), prompts (how you ask), problems (what actually needs solving), and practice (repeatable loops you can reuse). Bring a real project or admin task; leave with language for grants, collaborators, and your future self.
