**Hidden systems** can be named at different scales: DNS, moderation, rent, labor, geography. Pick one system you can diagram in five boxes.

### Sketch prompt

Make a static “systems map” page: five linked sections, each one plain-language explanation of a step in a process users rarely see (e.g. “what happens when you paste a URL”).
