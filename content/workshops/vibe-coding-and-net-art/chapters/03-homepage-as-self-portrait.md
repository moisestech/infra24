A **homepage** can read like a studio wall: fragments, lists, and “under construction” as aesthetic. Collect three traits you want a stranger to infer about your practice—then encode them without saying them literally.

### Sketch prompt

Build a one-page “self-portrait” with no photograph: only typography, lists, and one surprising `<details>` block.
