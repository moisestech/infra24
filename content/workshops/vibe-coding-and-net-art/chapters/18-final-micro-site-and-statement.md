This chapter aligns with the **capstone segment** of the live workshop: publish, peer feedback, and naming what you made.

### Checklist

- Public URL (GitHub Pages, Neocities, or CodePen—pick one path and finish it)
- `README` or footer with: tools used, what you directed vs what the machine suggested
- Statement: context, constraints, one reference to net art lineage (any chapter)
- Optional: link to the [resource appendix](/workshop/vibe-coding-and-net-art/resource-appendix) for citations

### Sketch prompt

Polish one prior sketch into a **coherent micro-site** (navigation + consistent type). Add a `/statement` section or anchor with your final text.
