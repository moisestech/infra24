Remix is a method, not a loophole. **Credit sources**, note what you transformed, and prefer rights-cleared or self-made fragments when publishing publicly.

### Sketch prompt

A “remix wall”: three found phrases (public domain text or your own past writing) rearranged into a new meaning. List sources at the bottom.
