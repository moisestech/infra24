Rozendaal’s browser works often hinge on **reduction + interaction**. Before complex JS, ask: what can `:hover`, transitions, and cursor alone do?

### Sketch prompt

One div, one class. Use only color, border-radius, and a `:hover` transition to make a “breathing” field that feels alive for ten seconds of attention.
