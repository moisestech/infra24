> **Lesson chrome:** Thesis, anchors, pathway chooser, comparison grid, tools, artifact, and reflection are driven by `lib/course/overlays/advanced-vibecoding-pathways.ts`. Use this markdown for extended reading; tune copy in the overlay when you refine pathways.

This chapter is intentionally **modular**: you are not meant to master CSS, p5.js, and three.js in one sitting. You are meant to **choose** one advanced direction, build a **small proof-of-concept**, and understand what that pathway optimizes for before you invest weeks.

## How to use this chapter

1. Skim the **pathway chooser** and **comparison grid** in the curated lesson view.
2. Pick **one** stack for a prototype.
3. Follow the official docs (MDN, p5.js, Processing overview, or the three.js manual) as your primary reference—not random tutorials.
4. Stop when the prototype **demonstrates one idea clearly**; defer polish to a later project.
