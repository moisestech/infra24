Napier-era works often foreground **what the browser is willing to do** (load, transform, leak). In a static-first workshop, simulate “behavior” with scroll, overflow, and `position` tricks before reaching for canvas.

### Sketch prompt

Build a page where scrolling reveals hidden layers (stacked sections or `position: sticky` surprise). Document one browser behavior you relied on.
