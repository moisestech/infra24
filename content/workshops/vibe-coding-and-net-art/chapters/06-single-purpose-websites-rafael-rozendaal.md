A **single-purpose site** has a clear contract: the visitor knows why they came. Name the one verb your page performs (soothe, confuse, celebrate, refuse).

### Sketch prompt

Ship a “single gesture” page: one primary interaction that changes the whole screen state. Document the gesture in one sentence for your workshop log.
