# Artist Website — Cheat Sheet

> **Pattern:** Collect → Organize → Write → Build → Publish → Improve  
> **Done by 1 PM:** structure + **one drafted page** (live URL is a bonus)

## 5 essential pages

| Page | Answers |
|------|---------|
| **Home** | Who + strongest image |
| **Work** | Main projects |
| **About** | Bio + statement |
| **CV / Press** | Proof of practice |
| **Contact** | How to reach you |

## No account vs account

| No account | Account-based |
|------------|---------------|
| Google Doc sitemap | Squarespace / Wix / Cargo |
| Canva mockup | Framer / Notion site |
| Copy in notes app | Draft in builder |

---

## 3 prompts (copy into ChatGPT or Claude)

**Homepage:** `Write 3 homepage intros for my artist site: very short, warm, and professional. I am based in [city], work with [media], explore [themes].`

**Project:** `Write 150-word and 50-word project descriptions. Title, year, materials, what viewer sees, why I made it.`

**Audit:** `Act as a curator. What is clear, confusing, missing? What should I fix first?`
